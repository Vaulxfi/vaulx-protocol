## Methodology notes

- **Domains**: Authoritative RDAP via Verisign (.com), Identity Digital (.io/.finance/.money), CentralNic (.xyz), Radix (.tech), Traficom (.fi), and TCP whois via whois.registry.co (.co). Accented names (`Pépètes`, `Graná`) were slugified (accents stripped) before checking — punycode domains remain unchecked and should be validated manually if kept.
- **RDAP status = "registered"** means a WHOIS record exists (domain is not freely available at a registrar). It does not mean the site is active.
- **Trademarks**: USPTO TESS, EUIPO eSearch, INPI-BR — looking at live registrations in Nice classes 9, 36, 42. Pending applications are flagged as RISK. Confirm with legal before filing anywhere.
- **Crypto conflicts**: CoinMarketCap, CoinGecko, DeFiLlama. Does not cover pre-launch projects or dormant listings.
- **SERP**: Google page 1 at time of check — rankings are volatile.
- **Social handles**: Existence of a profile page; some "TAKEN" profiles are dormant/inactive and may be recoverable via platform policy.
- **Brandability + fatal flags**: My qualitative assessment based on pronunciation in EN/PT/ES/FR, association strength, and phonetic clash with incumbents.

## Known data gaps

- `.co` responses were produced via TCP whois (port 43) — one occasional false "unclear" is possible; verify before purchase.
- `.solana` Freename availability is reported from web search results; the Freename platform itself rate-limited some queries.
- `.sol` SNS/Bonfida: where research returned `UNCLEAR`, it means the SNS aggregator APIs did not respond in time — does not imply taken.
